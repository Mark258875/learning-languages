# French Tenses

## Overview

| Tense | French Name | When to Use |
|-------|------------|-------------|
| Present | Présent | Current actions, general truths, near future |
| Near Future | Futur proche | Planned/immediate future |
| Simple Future | Futur simple | Future events |
| Passé composé | Passé composé | Completed past actions |
| Imperfect | Imparfait | Ongoing/habitual past, descriptions |
| Pluperfect | Plus-que-parfait | Past before another past event |
| Conditional | Conditionnel | Would do, polite requests |
| Subjunctive | Subjonctif | Doubt, emotion, necessity |

---

## Present Tense (Présent)

Used for:
- Actions happening now: *Je mange.* (I'm eating.)
- Habitual actions: *Je mange à midi.* (I eat at noon.)
- General truths: *Le soleil brille.* (The sun shines.)
- Near future (with time marker): *Je pars demain.* (I'm leaving tomorrow.)

---

## Near Future (Futur Proche)

**Formation:** aller (present) + infinitive

- *Je vais manger.* — I'm going to eat.
- *Tu vas partir?* — Are you going to leave?
- *Nous allons voyager.* — We're going to travel.

Most natural way to express the near future in spoken French.

---

## Passé Composé vs Imparfait

The most important distinction for past tense:

| Passé Composé | Imparfait |
|--------------|-----------|
| Completed action | Ongoing/background action |
| Specific time | Duration, habit |
| New events | Description, state |
| *Je suis allé au marché.* | *Il faisait beau.* |
| *Elle a mangé une pomme.* | *Quand j'étais enfant, je jouais dehors.* |

**Together:** Background (imparfait) interrupted by event (passé composé):
*Je dormais quand le téléphone a sonné.*
(I was sleeping [imparfait] when the phone rang [passé composé].)

---

## Conditional (Conditionnel)

**Formation:** futur stem + imparfait endings (-ais, -ais, -ait, -ions, -iez, -aient)

Used for:
- Hypothetical: *Je voudrais un café.* (I would like a coffee — polite)
- If-then with imparfait: *Si j'avais de l'argent, je voyagerais.* (If I had money, I would travel.)

---

## Subjunctive (Subjonctif)

Used after certain expressions of:
- **Wish/desire:** *Je veux que tu viennes.* (I want you to come.)
- **Emotion:** *Je suis content qu'il soit là.* (I'm happy he's here.)
- **Doubt:** *Je doute qu'il vienne.* (I doubt he'll come.)
- **Necessity:** *Il faut que tu étudies.* (You must study.)
- **After:** bien que (although), pour que (so that), avant que (before), à moins que (unless)

**Formation (present subjunctive):**
Take the ils/elles present form, drop -ent, add: -e, -es, -e, -ions, -iez, -ent

*parler* → parlent → parl- + e → *que je parle*

---

## Pluperfect (Plus-que-parfait)

**Formation:** avoir/être (imparfait) + past participle

Used for actions completed before another past action:
- *J'avais déjà mangé quand il est arrivé.*
  (I had already eaten when he arrived.)

## Common Time Expressions

| French | English | Use with |
|--------|---------|---------|
| maintenant | now | présent |
| aujourd'hui | today | présent, passé composé |
| hier | yesterday | passé composé |
| demain | tomorrow | futur |
| toujours | always | imparfait, présent |
| souvent | often | imparfait, présent |
| soudain | suddenly | passé composé |
| quand | when | both (context) |
| pendant que | while | imparfait |
