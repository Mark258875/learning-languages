# Russian Pronunciation

## Stress

Russian word stress is **free and unpredictable** — it must be memorized with each word.
Stress significantly changes vowel pronunciation (especially О and Е).

- Stressed syllables are pronounced clearly
- Unstressed syllables are reduced (особенно О → А)

*Example: молоко (milk) = ma-la-KO — only the final О is "oh", the first two are "ah"*

---

## Vowel Reduction

| Vowel | Stressed | Unstressed |
|-------|---------|------------|
| О | /o/ (round "oh") | /ɐ/ (like "a") |
| Е | /ɛ/ ("eh") | /ɪ/ (like short "i") |
| Я | /ja/ ("ya") | /jɪ/ (like "yi") |

---

## Consonant Softening (Palatalization)

Russian consonants can be **hard** or **soft** (palatalized):
- Hard: pronounced normally
- Soft: pronounced with tongue raised toward palate (like "y" glide)

Soft consonants occur:
- Before soft vowels (Е, Ё, И, Ю, Я)
- Before the soft sign (Ь)

**Slovak comparison:** Similar to the difference between *l* and *ľ*, *n* and *ň* in Slovak!

---

## Voiced/Voiceless Consonant Pairs

| Voiced | Voiceless | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Б /b/ | П /p/ | |
| В /v/ | Ф /f/ | |
| Г /g/ | К /k/ | |
| Д /d/ | Т /t/ | |
| Ж /ʐ/ | Ш /ʂ/ | |
| З /z/ | С /s/ | |

**Devoicing at word-end:** Voiced consonants become voiceless at the end of a word:
- хлеб → /xlʲep/ (not /xlʲeb/)
- год → /got/ (not /god/)
- нож → /noʂ/ (not /noʐ/)

**Assimilation:** A voiced consonant before a voiceless one becomes voiceless (and vice versa):
- сделать → /zdʲelɐtʲ/ (c becomes z before d)

---

## The Letter Р

Russian Р is an **alveolar trill** — very similar to Slovak R!
Practice: *рыба* (fish), *работа* (work), *Россия* (Russia)

---

## Hard vs Soft Ш and Ж

- **Ш** and **Ж** are always hard in Russian (unlike Slovak Š which can be soft)
- They never palatalize, even before И or Е
- *шить* = /ʂɨtʲ/ (to sew) — Ш stays hard

---

## The Letter Ы

One of the hardest sounds for non-Slavic speakers. For Slovak speakers — think of a very dark *i*, almost like *y* in English "rhythm":
- *ты* (you) = /tɨ/
- *рыба* (fish) = /ˈrɨbə/

---

## Spelling Rules

1. After Г, К, Х, Ж, Ш, Щ, Ч — write И not Ы
2. After Ж, Ш, Щ, Ч — write А not Я, У not Ю
3. After Ж, Ш, Щ, Ч, Ц — write Е not О (in unstressed position)
